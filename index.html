What is Smart City?

Internet Of Things

      Does this Smart City have anything to do with IoT? Yes, 100 percent is closely related: IoT is one of the technology tools that can be used for Smart City application development.

      Such a system is very interesting and will be very useful if it can be applied in all regions in Indonesia, not limited to urban areas only. But the technology requires a large enough CAPEX in the form of infrastructure and hardware. Therefore, the "barrier to implementation" or obstacle to implementing this technology is much higher.

All Based on Smart People

      Smart People is a large number of people living in the same region and have a relatively similar culture that can understand the benefits and uses of the infrastructure facilities that governments have provided in their respective environments.           

      The community is also required to participate actively in the management and administration of the city, as well as being an active city user. Society is also the most determining factor in the success or failure of the creation of smart city. one of the containers for the creation of smart city is education.
